Our Commitment to Diversity

At Slap, we strive to create a workplace that embraces diversity, equity, and inclusion. While our leadership team appears to be a vision of consistency, we are dedicated to fostering diverse perspectives across all levels of our organization. We are proud that 75% of our leadership team comes from religious minority backgrounds. We know there’s work to be done, and we’re here to learn, listen, and grow—one step at a time.
